OUR STORY

The Wizards Wiffle Ball Club

Founded in 2025, the Wizards Wiffle Ball Club was created with a simple mission: to bring the joy of wiffle ball to players of all ages and skill levels in our community.

What started as a small group of friends playing in a backyard has grown into a vibrant community of wiffle ball enthusiasts who gather regularly to enjoy this classic American game.

  • Family-friendly environment for all ages
  • Inclusive games that welcome players of all skill levels
  • Regular games and special events throughout the year
  • Custom-built field modeled after Fenway Park

Our club focuses on creating a fun, inclusive environment where the spirit of the game and good sportsmanship are always the priority.

Our Mission

What drives the Wizards Wiffle Ball Club

Community

We aim to build a strong community of wiffle ball enthusiasts who share a passion for the game and enjoy spending time together.

Inclusivity

We welcome players of all ages, backgrounds, and skill levels, ensuring everyone has a chance to participate and enjoy the game.

Fun

Above all, we believe in having fun. While we may keep score, the real victory is in the joy of playing and the memories we create together.
