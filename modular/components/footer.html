About Wiffle Ball

Wiffle ball is a variation of baseball designed for indoor or outdoor play in confined areas. The game is played using a perforated, lightweight, rubbery plastic ball and a long, hollow plastic bat.

Our club focuses on creating a fun, inclusive environment for players of all ages and skill levels.
