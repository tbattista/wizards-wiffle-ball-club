General

  • Game is designed for hit and run scoring
  • Ties/arguments/fouls will always go to the batter and or offence
  • Have fun score runs

Batting Rules

  • Only adults can strike out
  • Swing and miss = strike 1 or 2
  • Foul ball = strike 1 or 2
  • Third strike = swing and miss or hit the strike target
  • No walks or hit-by-pitch

Pitching Rules

  • Ball speed must be controlled and over the plate
  • Pitch speed should match the age/skill of batter
  • Pitchers will be warned for too much speed or inaccuracy

Fielding & Outs

  • Force outs by tagging the base
  • Tag outs by tagging the runner with the ball
