Game Setup

Everything you need to know to get started with our wiffle ball games

Game Setup

  • This is a fun ALL AGES game. (must be able to hit and run bases alone)
  • 5 Inning Game
  • 3 outs per inning
  • No gloves
  • Bats and balls provided
  • All bats must be standard yellow plastic wiffle ball bats (taped/customized is fine)

General

  • This game is designed for more hits and runs
  • Ties/arguments/fouls will always go to the batter and or offence
  • Have fun, score runs

Batting Rules

  • Only adults can strike out
  • No called strikes
  • Swing and miss = strike 1 or 2
  • Foul ball = strike 1 or 2
  • Third strike = swing and miss or hit the strike target
  • No walks or hit-by-pitch

Pitching Rules

  • Kids under 12 can use a glove as a pitcher
  • Pitches are ment to be hit
  • Ball speed must be controlled and over the plate
  • Pitch speed should match the age/skill of batter
  • Pitchers will be warned or replaced for too much speed or inaccuracy

Fielding & Outs

  • Force outs by tagging the base
  • Tag outs by tagging the runner with the ball
  • Foul ball will be normal base lines, and 10ft in front of home
  • No full time catcher
  • Fly ball outs all runners back to base free and safe - run on any hit, no tagging up
  • Kids under 5 get a free base on all hits (fake play)

About Wiffle Ball

Wiffle ball is a variation of baseball designed for indoor or outdoor play in confined areas. The game is played using a perforated, lightweight, rubbery plastic ball and a long, hollow plastic bat.

Our club focuses on creating a fun, inclusive environment for players of all ages and skill levels.
