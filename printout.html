Wizards Wiffle Ball Club

Official Game Day Rules & Scoring Sheet — 2026 Season

Field: West Fenway Park Season: 2026

General Rules

  • Game is designed for hit and run scoring
  • Ties / arguments / fouls will always go to the batter and/or offense
  • Have fun — score runs

Game Setup

  • Fun ALL AGES game (must be at least 6)
  • 5 inning game
  • 3 outs per inning
  • No gloves
  • Bats and balls provided
  • Standard yellow plastic wiffle ball bats only (taped/customized is fine)

Batting

  • Only adults can strike out
  • Swing and miss = strike 1 or 2
  • Foul ball = strike 1 or 2
  • Third strike = swing and miss or hit the strike target
  • No walks or hit-by-pitch

Pitching

  • Ball speed must be controlled and over the plate
  • Pitch speed should match age/skill of batter
  • Pitchers will be warned for too much speed or inaccuracy
  • Each pitcher pitches only 1 inning
  • Pitchers must alternate between a kid and an adult each inning

Fielding

  • Force outs by tagging the base
  • Tag outs by tagging the runner with the ball
